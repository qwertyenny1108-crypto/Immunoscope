About ImmunoScope
An AI-powered immunology research intelligence tool for researchers, clinicians, and students.

🔍 How it works

Each topic triggers a Claude AI web search across recent journals, preprints, and science news. The AI synthesizes findings into structured, readable briefs with citations.

🔑 Your API key

Your Anthropic API key is stored only in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device. Get a key at console.anthropic.com.

📚 Coverage areas

Cancer immunotherapy, autoimmune diseases, vaccine science, innate & adaptive immunity, cytokine biology, and immune signaling pathways.

AI-generated summaries are for research reference only. Always verify against original publications before clinical or research application.
